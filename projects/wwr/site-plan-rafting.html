Overview

Purpose

High adrenaline and real-time information. That's the purpose of our website - to show you the benefits for body, spirit and happiness in rafting with us.

Audience

Our audience is mostly young families and people with a spirit of adventure. Age between 15 and 40, looking for something new and challenging. Normally, my audience accesses our site through cell phones or laptops.
